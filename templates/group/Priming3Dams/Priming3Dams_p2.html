Overview

Imagine that you are part of a committee that has to decide which one of three existing dams you would want to repair. Naturally, you should repair the dam that has the highest chance to fail.

You will be given some information about the current status of each dam. You will then decide on your own which dam to repair. After making your decision, you will be randomly and anonymously placed in a group of three people. Your group will discuss and decide which dam to repair through anonymous voting. The chosen dam will be the one that receives two or more votes (In the case that each dam receives one vote, a dam will be randomly chosen for your group).

{% if payment_flag %}

After completing the tasks, you will be paid a participation fee plus some additional earnings based on your decisions and your group's decisions.
