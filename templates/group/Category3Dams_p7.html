Procedure

You will go through the following steps:

  • Step 1. You will be given some information about the presence of conditions A, B, C, D, E, and F at Dam 1, Dam 2, and Dam 3. Different participants may receive different information.
  • Step 2. You will be given 5 minutes to decide which dam you should repair.
  • Step 3. You will be randomly and anonymously placed in a group of three participants.
  • Step 4. Your group will discuss the following questions one by one using an online chat box:
    1. What are the conditions present at Dam 1?
    2. What are the conditions present at Dam 2?
    3. What are the conditions present at Dam 3?
    4. Which dam should you repair?
    Note: Your group can discuss up to 5 minutes for each question. However, feel free to end the discussion earlier. After the group discussion of a question, each participant will answer the corresponding question on your own screen.
  • Step 5. Once the group discussion ends, each participant of your group will vote anonymously on which dam to repair. The dam chosen by your group will be the one that receives two or more votes (In the case that each dam receives one vote, a dam will be randomly chosen for your group).

Payoff

Your total earning = $5 participation fee + additional earnings.

Your additional earnings are the sum of the following two items:

1. Given your information, if you make the correct conclusion about whether a condition is present or not at a dam, you earn an extra $1. That is, if you make the correct conclusion about all six conditions at the three dams, you earn an extra $1 x 18 = $18.

2. If your group has chosen the correct dam to repair, each participant in your group earns an extra $5.


As soon as your group makes a decision, you will be directed to complete a survey. After completing your survey, you will collect your earnings in private and be directed to leave the lab.
